About Ted

Ted Healey is a seasoned IT executive and entrepreneur with over 25 years of experience in software engineering, data science, and solutions development for Fortune 1000 companies. As the co-founder of a startup acquired by Thomson Reuters, Ted has demonstrated a unique ability to innovate and drive growth. He has extensive experience building and managing high-performing teams across the globe, including in the US, China, the Philippines, Poland, and Australia.
Ted's leadership roles have spanned a variety of sectors, including financial services, healthcare IoT, and corporate real estate analytics. His technical expertise encompasses product development, cloud computing, and big data systems.
Ted excels in strategic planning, technical architecture, and fostering collaborative environments that promote excellence and innovation.
